About Rover Control Panel

The Rover Control Panel is a web application designed to remotely control and monitor our Arduino Uno-based rover equipped with an HC-05 Bluetooth module. With this control panel, you can explore and maneuver the rover in real-time over Bluetooth.

Our rover is equipped with various sensors that provide valuable feedback, such as ultrasonic sensors for distance measurement and a camera for capturing images along its path. The sensor feedback is displayed in the control panel, giving you a comprehensive view of the rover's surroundings.

How to Use:

  1. Make sure the Arduino Uno and the rover are powered on and the Bluetooth module is connected.
  2. Access the Rover Control Panel from any device with a web browser.
  3. Use the buttons and sliders in the control panel to direct the rover's movement (forward, backward, left, right).
  4. Observe the sensor feedback section to get real-time updates about the rover's status and surroundings.

We hope you enjoy using the Rover Control Panel to explore and interact with our rover. Feel free to contact us if you have any feedback or suggestions for improvement.
